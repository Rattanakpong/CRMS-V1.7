Healthcare for Refugees in Cambodia

Introduction

We are committed to delivering comprehensive healthcare services to refugees in Cambodia. Our goal is to ensure that everyone, regardless of their background, has access to the medical care they need. We focus on providing equitable healthcare services that cover a range of needs, from routine check-ups to specialized treatments.

We believe that every refugee deserves quality healthcare. If you would like to support our mission or learn more about our services, please contact us or donate.

Adult Healthcare Services

We provide comprehensive healthcare services for adult refugees, including regular check-ups, mental health support, and emergency care. All services are provided without discrimination based on race, gender, religion, or nationality.

Children's Healthcare Services

Our healthcare services for children include vaccinations, growth and development monitoring, and treatment for common childhood illnesses. We aim to provide a safe and healthy environment for the younger members of the refugee community. Every child is welcome and cared for without any form of discrimination.

Women's Health Services

We offer specialized healthcare services for women, including prenatal and postnatal care, family planning, and support for survivors of gender-based violence. Our team is committed to ensuring the health and well-being of women refugees. All services are provided with respect and without discrimination.

Mental Health Services

Mental health is a critical aspect of overall well-being. We provide counseling, therapy, and support groups for refugees dealing with trauma, anxiety, depression, and other mental health issues. Our goal is to help individuals build resilience and cope with their experiences. We believe in providing equal access to mental health services for everyone.
