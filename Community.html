Community Integration

Cambodia Refugee & Migrant Support (CRMS)

Cambodia Refugee & Migrant Support (CRMS) Community Services partners with refugees, asylees, immigrants and survivors of trafficking to address their needs by connecting them to their new community, providing education and employment services and the help they need to succeed in their new country.



If you would like to help participants with their acclimation and stability, please consider purchasing essential resources through the Refugee Immigrant and Community Services wish list Here.

Activity In Community

Cultural Performances

Schedule dance, music, and theatrical performances from both immigrant and local Cambodian groups. Include traditional and contemporary acts to appeal to a broad audience.

Food Stalls

Set up food stalls offering traditional dishes from different immigrant communities and local Cambodian cuisine. Encourage attendees to sample a variety of foods.

Art and Craft Exhibits

Display artwork and crafts from both immigrants and local artists. Include interactive art projects where attendees can contribute to a collective piece.

Cultural Fashion Show

Host a fashion show featuring traditional clothing from various cultures. Encourage participants to explain the significance of their attire.

Interactive Games and Sports

Set up games and sports that are popular in different cultures, such as soccer, traditional Cambodian games, and games from immigrant cultures. Organize friendly matches and mixed teams to encourage interaction.

Workshops and Demonstrations

Organize workshops on traditional crafts, cooking classes, and language lessons. Include demonstrations of traditional ceremonies or rituals from various cultures.
